Why It Matters

Most small businesses don't have a people problem. They have an HR infrastructure problem and just don't know it yet.

Without the right foundation in place, small problems compound quietly. A misclassified employee. An outdated handbook. A hiring process that relies on gut feeling. A benefits enrollment that falls apart mid-cycle. These are manageable until they aren't.

Good HR doesn't require a full-time team. It requires the right person who has done this before and knows what matters at your size. That's exactly what 1:1 Consulting was built to provide.

Small organizations deserve real HR support, sized to fit how they actually operate, not how a large enterprise does.
